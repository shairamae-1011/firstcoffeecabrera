ABOUT

Here at But First, Coffee, we serve handcrafted coffee and freshly baked pastries made with the finest ingredients. Whether you're looking for a morning pick-me-up, a cozy place to unwind, or a sweet treat to brighten your day, we’ve got you covered.

Whether you’re stopping by for a quick cup, settling in with a book, or catching up with friends, we’re here to make every sip and bite memorable. Our café is more than just a place to grab a drink—it’s a warm, inviting space where people can connect, relax, and savor every bite and sip. Come in, grab a cup, and let’s make today delicious!


SERVICES

We’re dedicated to providing more than just exceptional coffee and pastries—we create an experience. Whether you’re looking for a cozy space to enjoy your morning brew, a quick grab-and-go option, or catering for a special event, we have you covered. Our café offers both dine-in and takeout services, allowing you to enjoy our handcrafted drinks and freshly baked pastries at your own pace. For those on the go, we provide convenient online ordering and pickup, making it easier than ever to get your favorite treats.
